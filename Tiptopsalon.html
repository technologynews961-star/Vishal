About Us

Welcome to Tip Top Family Salon & Beauty

Located in the heart of Parel Village, Mumbai, we are dedicated to providing a premium grooming and relaxation experience for the entire family. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that every visit leaves you looking and feeling your absolute best.

From contemporary haircuts to rejuvenating spa therapies, we use top-tier products and maintain the highest standards of hygiene and customer care.
